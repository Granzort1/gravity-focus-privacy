Gravity Focus ("App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Google account profile information (name, profile photo)
  • Tasks, routines, schedules, and focus session data you create
  • App settings and preferences

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App version
  • Usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash logs and error reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Provide task management, routines, focus sessions, and scheduling features
  • Account Management: User authentication and data synchronization across devices
  • Subscription Management: Verify and manage Pro subscription status
  • Advertising: Display personalized ads to free users
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns and fix errors
  • Support: Respond to inquiries and provide customer support

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

Account Deletion: Upon request, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
