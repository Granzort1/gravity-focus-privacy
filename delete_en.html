Gravity Focus Account Deletion

한국어 버전

Deleting your Gravity Focus account will permanently remove all your data. This page explains how to delete your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Google account connection
  • Tasks, routines, and schedules
  • Pomodoro/focus session history
  • App settings and preferences
  • Google Drive backup data (if used)
  • Subscription information (subscription itself must be cancelled separately)
Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. Please backup any data you need before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Gravity Focus app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Navigate to Profile section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Read the confirmation message and tap Confirm Delete

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via email.

Email: skadldh@gmail.com

Email Template:

Subject: [Gravity Focus] Account Deletion Request

Body:
Hello,

I would like to request the deletion of my Gravity Focus account.

- Registered Email: [your email]
- Reason (optional): [your reason]

Thank you.

Processing Time

  • In-app deletion: Immediate
  • Email request: Within 30 days of request

Subscription Cancellation

Account deletion and subscription cancellation are separate. If you have a Pro subscription, you must cancel it separately to avoid future charges.

  • Android: Google Play Store → Subscriptions → Gravity Focus → Cancel Subscription

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact skadldh@gmail.com.
